SMT-COMP 2018 is organized under the direction of the SMT Steering Committee. The organizing team is

  • Matthias Heizmann - University of Freiburg, Germany (co-organizer)
  • Aina Niemetz - Standford University, USA (co-organizer)
  • Giles Reger - University of Manchester, UK (co-organizer)
  • Tjark Weber - Uppsala University, Sweden (chair)

The competition uses the StarExec service, which is hosted at the University of Iowa.

The SMT-COMP web site is hosted on SourceForge, at smtcomp.sourceforge.net.
