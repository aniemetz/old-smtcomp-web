The 2014 edition of the Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP) is part of the SMT 2014 workshop, associated with SAT 2014 at the Vienna Summer of Logic (June 30 - July 17-18, 2014, Vienna, AU). The SMT workshop will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the SMTCOMP competition.
