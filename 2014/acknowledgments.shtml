SMT-COMP 2014 is co-organized by:

  • David Cok : GrammaTech, USA
  • David Deharbe : Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte, Brazil

The competition uses the STAR-EXEC server, which is hosted at The University of Iowa.

The U.S. National Science Foundation, under grant CNS-0551697 supported the earlier SMTExec computational cluster.

The SMTCOMP web site is hosted on sourceforge, at smtcomp.sourceforge.net, currently maintained by David Cok.
