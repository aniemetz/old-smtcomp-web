The 2015 edition of the Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP) (July 19, 2015) is part of the SMT Workshop 2015, associated with CAV 2015. The competitors and results of the competition will be presented at the SMT Workshop and at the main CAV conference.

The organizers of SMT-COMP 2015 are Sylvain Conchon, David Deharbe, and Tjark Weber.
