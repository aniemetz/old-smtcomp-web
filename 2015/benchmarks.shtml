The SMT Competition will use a subset of the benchmarks available within the 2015-06-01 release of SMT-LIB, as described in the competition rules. Specifically, benchmarks with unknown status, and benchmarks that contain partial bit-vector operations (e.g., division), will not be used.

The SMT-LIB benchmarks are available on StarExec, in space root/SMT/SMT-LIB benchmarks/2015-06-01. They may also be downloaded from http://www.cs.nyu.edu/~barrett/smtlib/.

The subset of benchmarks eligible for the SMT Competition is available on StarExec, in spaces root/SMT/Competitions and evaluations/SMT-COMP 2015/Main Track and root/SMT/Competitions and evaluations/SMT-COMP 2015/Application Track for the main and application track, respectively.
