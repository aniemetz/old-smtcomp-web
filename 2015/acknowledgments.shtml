SMT-COMP 2015 is organized under the direction of the SMT Steering Committee. The organizing team is

  • Sylvain Conchon - Paris-Sud University, France
  • David Deharbe - Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte, Brazil
  • Tjark Weber - Uppsala University, Sweden

The competition uses the StarExec service, which is hosted at the University of Iowa.

The SMT-COMP web site is hosted on SourceForge, at smtcomp.sourceforge.net.
