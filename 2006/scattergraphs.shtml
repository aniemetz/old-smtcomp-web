• Each point represents a benchmark.
  • The running times are in seconds.
  • Points above (below) the diagonal correspond to benchmarks where the system on the x (y) axis is faster than the other.
  • Points one division above are an order of magnitude faster.
  • A scatter that is shallower (steeper) than the diagonal indicates the performance of the system on the x (y) axis deteriorates relative to the other as benchmark complexity increases.
