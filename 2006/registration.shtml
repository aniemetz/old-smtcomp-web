System pre-registration and submission is now open.
  • Pre-Registration: submit an email to all organizers (Clark Barrett, Leonardo de Moura, Aaron Stump). The subject must be smt-comp pre-registration. The email must contain the system name, authors and their affiliations. The email should also specify in which divisions you are planning to participate. It is not necessary to submit the system binaries or source code.
  • System Submission: submit an email to all organizers (Clark Barrett, Leonardo de Moura, Aaron Stump). The subject must be smt-comp system submission. The email must contain a tarball with the system (sources or Linux executable). The tarball must include a README on compilation (if necessary) and installation; as well as a 1-2 page system description, and a magic number for pseudo-random selection of benchmarks. Please also indicate whether or not you will be available to give a short presentation about your system at the August 20th evening session for SMT-COMP. Deadline: 8th of August.
