• Pre-Registration: The competitors must notify the organizers they are planning to enter the competition. The notification should be performed by email. It is not necessary to submit the system binaries or source code. Deadline: 15th of May.
  • System Submission: The competitors must email the organizers a tarball with their system (sources or Linux executable). The tarball must include a README on compilation (if necessary) and installation; as well as a 1-2 page system description as explained in the rules relating to Entrants. Deadline: 1st of July.
  • Beginning of the Competition: 6th of July
